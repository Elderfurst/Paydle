Welcome to Paydle!

The goal of the game is to guess the salary of a given profession.

After each guess additional information will be revelead to you about the job as well as a hint to how close you are.
Each of the jobs is a specific job, so it pertains to one single person, you might see two jobs with the same title have vastly different data points.

Guess within 5% of the correct salary to win!
